Amrutham Ventures Private Limited is a Business Venture lead by young enterprising couples. In early 2014, they registered the company under Registrar of Companies Ernakulam. They entered the market by distributing leading brands of Fast-Moving Consumer goods. Then company also embarked on the trading of Spices, Dals & Selected List from Groceries.

Amrutham Ventures can fulfil every statutory requirement, infrastructure & resources as a distributor as well as a trader or for export import.Clear Cut Strategies have been designed and implemented by the management becoming one of the indispensible companies in FMCG as well as Spices in Indian Market.The company focus on Spices, Fresh Fruits & Vegetables for Exporting while importing Major International Brands in FMCG along with Arabian Spices

ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

Amrutham Ventures Private Limited, incorporated in 2014, is a company dealing with FMCG products, Grocery & Milk During the early days, the prime focus was in the distribution of proven brands in FMCG. The company penetrated the market with a systematic approach. The roaring success in business gave confidence for expanding their business. Thus, the company expanded its operations by adding spices. The next expansion was with selected products from groceries & milk.

The company is catering to almost all major supermarket chains across Kerala. Further, Major Bakery, Hotels and Hospitals are also business partners of the company. The clients are pretty happy with the delivery network as well as the sales team of the company. The outstanding marketing team in Amrutham Ventures is exploring new markets. Amrutham Ventures have a proven track of professional business with best practices. The perfect blend of the visionary management, dedicated work team and the systematic approach is the secret of phenomenal success of the enterprise.
